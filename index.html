About

Resiste...
REally SImple Syndication To Email

The purpose of Resiste is to be an all-purpose RSS-to-email client. Schedule Resiste to run at an interval of your choosing (15 minutes, 1 hour, etc.). When Resiste runs, it will:

  1. Loop over each RSS/ATOM feed you've configured
  2. Determine the new posts since it last ran
  3. Email you the new posts

Why

You want an application like Resiste because it's the difference between pulling information and pushing information. RSS was created to pull information. Unfortunately, most email clients no longer support RSS. I basically check only one thing all day, and that's my email. If it comes to my inbox (i.e it's "pushed" to me) I'll read it and respond. However if it doesn't come to my inbox, it's very unlikely I'll remember to go get it (i.e. "pull" it to me). So this is why I created Resiste - to automatically "push" new posts to my inbox.
