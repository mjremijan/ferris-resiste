About

Resiste is an acronym that stands for:

Really Simple Syndication To Email

How It Works

  1. Loop over each RSS/ATOM feed you've configured
  2. Determine the new posts since it last ran
  3. Email you the new posts

Modules

ferris-resiste-console is a 100% pure Java stand-alone and self-contained RSS-to-email application (it even comes with its own JRE).

Only 3 simple steps to get up and running:

  1. Configure your email account
  2. Configure the RSS/ATOM feeds
  3. Schedule the application to run as often as you want
